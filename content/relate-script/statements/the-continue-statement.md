

- The continue Statement

---
**Syntax:**
**continue**

**Description:**
The continue statement must be inside a statement block that is the body-statement of a looping statement. When the continue statement is executed, any statements after it are skipped, and the loop's condition is immediately evaluated to determine if looping should continue (except in the case of a for loop where the updaters are still executed before the condition is re-evaluated).
